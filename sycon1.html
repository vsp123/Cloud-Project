‘Creating leaders and inspiring change’.


Sri Sivasubramaniya Nadar College of Engineering (SSNCE), popularly known as SSN College of Engineering or simply SSN, is an engineering institution located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. The college is certified to ISO 9001:2000 standard[1] by the National Board of Accreditation. In March 2018, the college was granted autonomous status by UGC.It has sophisticated hostel facilities with a world class mess and room management services.
