Gongora(Sorrel Leaves) Pickle

INGREDIENTS

  • 7-8 bunches of gongura leaves red stem/sorrel leaves
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt - to taste
  • 2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 medium size tomato chopped
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  • 3-4 garlic cloves
  • ½ cup water
  • For Tempering:
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • ½ tsp urad dal(split black gram)
  • ½ tsp chana dal(bengal gram/chick pea gram)
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp finely chopped garlic
  • 4-5 dried red chillies
  • 5-6 curry leaves


PROCEDURE

  1. Firstly, take a cooking vessel or a pressure cooker. Into it wash and add the gongura leaves, add salt, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, garlic cloves. Also add green chillies, coriander leaves, chopped onion and chopped tomato.
  2. Add little oil, add little water(1/4th cup) and pressure cook until the leaves get soft or pressure cook for two whistles. Reduce the steam, cool it and blend it into a paste or mash it well.
  3. For Tempering
  4. In a sauce pan, add oil and heat it. Add urad dal, chana dal and saute. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped garlic.
  5. Add dried red chillies and stir well. Add curry leaves and when they give out a crackling sound transfer the tadka into the gongura chutney/ambada/ambadey ki chutney. Cut onions into small cubes and mix in the chutney and enjoy it with hot hot rice!!!
